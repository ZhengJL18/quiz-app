# 阅读综合练习（一）

## 核心知识点

本练习聚焦四六级阅读三大题型：**选词填空**、**长篇匹配**、**仔细阅读**，以及段落翻译。重点考查**词汇搭配**、**逻辑衔接**、**段落主旨提取**及**中英转换能力**。

## 重点难点

- **选词填空**：辨析近义词、固定搭配，根据上下文语法与语义判断词性及词义。
- **长篇匹配**：快速定位段落关键词，区分细节与主旨，避免被干扰项误导。
- **仔细阅读**：理解隐含信息，区分事实与观点，推理作者态度。
- **翻译**：处理中文四字格、无主句、主动变被动等结构差异，确保英文表达地道。

## 示例题目

### 一、选词填空（每题1分，共10分）

从方框中选择恰当单词填入空白处，每词限用一次。

|  |  |  |  |  |
|--|--|--|--|--|
| A. emerge | B. significant | C. impact | D. adopt | E. primarily |
| F. approach | G. traditional | H. gradually | I. despite | J. potential |

**The Rise of E-learning**

Online education has become a ____ (1) force in modern learning. Many institutions ____ (2) this new method to reach more students. ____ (3) its initial challenges, e-learning continues to grow. It allows learners to ____ (4) new skills at their own pace. This ____ (5) not only saves time but also reduces costs.

**参考答案**：1. B (significant)  2. D (adopt)  3. I (despite)  4. J (potential)  5. C (impact)

### 二、长篇匹配（每题2分，共10分）

将下列句子与对应段落匹配（段落略）。

1. The author emphasizes the role of technology in reducing educational inequality.
2. Online platforms offer personalized learning experiences.

### 三、仔细阅读（每题2分，共10分）

**Passage** (节选)

> The shift toward renewable energy is not merely an environmental necessity but also an economic opportunity. Countries that invest early in solar and wind technologies are likely to gain a competitive edge in the global market. However, the transition requires substantial upfront costs and policy support.

**问题**：What does the author imply about early investment in renewable energy?

A. It guarantees immediate profit.  
B. It is only beneficial for wealthy nations.  
C. It may lead to long-term advantages.  
D. It is too risky for most economies.

**参考答案**：C

### 四、段落翻译（中译英，共20分）

**原文**：随着人工智能技术的发展，许多传统行业正在经历深刻变革。企业不仅需要提高生产效率，还要关注数据隐私和伦理问题。政府也在积极制定相关法规，以平衡创新与安全。

**参考译文**：With the development of artificial intelligence technology, many traditional industries are undergoing profound changes. Companies not only need to improve production efficiency but also pay attention to data privacy and ethical issues. Governments are also actively formulating relevant regulations to balance innovation and security.

## 练习题

### Q1 (fill_blank)
选词填空：从方框中选择合适的词填入空格中（每个词仅用一次）。\n\nThe rapid development of artificial intelligence has ______ (1) many industries, from healthcare to finance. Some experts believe that AI will eventually ______ (2) human workers in repetitive tasks, while others argue that it will create new job opportunities. \n\n词库：A. transformed  B. replace  C. enhance  D. eliminate

答案：['A', 'B']

### Q2 (multiple_choice)
长篇匹配：将以下段落与对应的标题匹配。\n\n段落1：The Great Wall of China, stretching over 13,000 miles, was built over centuries to protect Chinese states and empires from invasions. It is now a UNESCO World Heritage site and a symbol of Chinese civilization.\n\n标题选项：\nA. A Symbol of National Pride\nB. Historical Defense Mechanism\nC. Modern Tourist Attraction\nD. Engineering Marvel

- [x] A. A
- [x] B. B
- [ ] C. C
- [ ] D. D

### Q3 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：根据短文内容选择最佳答案。\n\nPassage: The sharing economy has grown rapidly in recent years, with companies like Uber and Airbnb leading the way. However, critics argue that these platforms often bypass local regulations and labor laws, leading to unfair competition and exploitation of workers.\n\nQuestion: What is the main concern about the sharing economy according to the passage?

- [ ] A. A. It is not profitable for companies.
- [x] B. B. It may violate local laws and harm workers.
- [ ] C. C. It only benefits wealthy individuals.
- [ ] D. D. It is difficult for consumers to use.

### Q4 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：根据短文内容选择最佳答案。\n\nPassage: According to a recent study, regular physical exercise can significantly reduce the risk of depression. The researchers found that even moderate activities like walking for 30 minutes a day can improve mental health.\n\nQuestion: What does the study suggest about exercise?

- [ ] A. A. Only intense exercise is beneficial.
- [ ] B. B. It has no effect on mental health.
- [x] C. C. Even light exercise can help prevent depression.
- [ ] D. D. It is only effective for young people.

### Q5 (short_answer)
翻译题：将以下中文段落翻译成英文。\n\n中国近年来在高铁技术方面取得了巨大成就。截至2023年，中国高铁运营里程已超过4万公里，位居世界第一。高铁不仅缩短了城市间的距离，也促进了经济发展和旅游业的繁荣。


### Q6 (short_answer)
翻译题：将以下中文段落翻译成英文。\n\n春节是中华民族最重要的传统节日，象征着团圆和新的开始。人们会贴春联、放鞭炮、吃年夜饭，并给晚辈发红包。近年来，越来越多的人选择在春节期间出国旅游，体验不同的文化。


### Q7 (fill_blank)
选词填空：从方框中选择合适的词填入空格中（每个词仅用一次）。\n\nMany students find it difficult to ______ (1) their time effectively during exam periods. To improve efficiency, experts suggest breaking study sessions into 25-minute intervals, known as the Pomodoro Technique. This method helps maintain ______ (2) and reduce stress.\n\n词库：A. manage  B. concentration  C. procrastinate  D. memorize

答案：['A', 'B']
