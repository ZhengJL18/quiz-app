# 阅读综合练习（二）

## 核心知识点

本章聚焦四六级阅读与翻译的常见题型，重点训练**信息定位**、**逻辑推断**与**段落翻译**能力。阅读部分涵盖选词填空、长篇匹配、仔细阅读；翻译部分为中译英段落翻译。

## 重点难点

- **选词填空**：根据词性（名词、动词、形容词、副词）缩小选项范围，结合上下文逻辑（因果、转折、并列）确定答案。
- **长篇匹配**：快速扫读题干关键词（专有名词、数字、大写字母），回原文定位同义替换句，注意段落首尾句。
- **仔细阅读**：区分主旨题、细节题、推断题，避免过度推理，选项常为原文的**同义改写**。
- **翻译**：处理中文无主句、长定语、文化负载词，优先使用简单句确保语法正确。

## 简单示例

### 选词填空示例

原文：The new policy aims to ______ economic growth while reducing inequality.  
选项：A. accelerate（加速） B. acceleration（加速，名词） C. accelerated（加速，过去式）  
解析：空格前为 `to`，后接动词原形，选 A。

### 长篇匹配示例

题干：How did the company respond to the environmental crisis?  
原文段落：...The firm immediately launched a recycling program to address the pollution issue.  
匹配：`respond` 对应 `launched`，`environmental crisis` 对应 `pollution issue`。

### 仔细阅读示例

问题：What is the main cause of the decline in bee populations?  
原文：The widespread use of pesticides has been identified as a key factor.  
答案：Pesticides.（细节题，直接定位关键词）

### 翻译示例

中文：中国高铁网络覆盖全国，极大缩短了城市间的旅行时间。  
翻译：China's high-speed rail network covers the entire country, significantly reducing travel time between cities.  
解析：`覆盖全国` 译为 `covers the entire country`；`缩短` 译为 `reducing`，用现在分词表结果。

## 练习题

### Q1 (fill_blank)
选词填空：从方框中选择合适的单词填入空白处。
原文：The rapid development of artificial intelligence has ______ (1) many industries, from healthcare to finance. It is expected to ______ (2) our daily lives in the coming decades.
选项：A. transformed  B. transform  C. transforming  D. transformation

答案：A

### Q2 (fill_blank)
选词填空：从方框中选择合适的单词填入空白处。
原文：Climate change is one of the most ______ (1) challenges facing humanity. Governments around the world are taking ______ (2) to reduce carbon emissions.
选项：A. urgent  B. urgency  C. urgently  D. urge

答案：A

### Q3 (single_choice)
长篇匹配：请将以下段落与对应的标题匹配。
段落：Many young people today prefer to work in the gig economy rather than take traditional full-time jobs. They value flexibility and autonomy over job security.
标题选项：
A. The Rise of Remote Work
B. The Changing Nature of Employment
C. The Benefits of Higher Education
D. The Impact of Automation

- [ ] A. A. The Rise of Remote Work
- [x] B. B. The Changing Nature of Employment
- [ ] C. C. The Benefits of Higher Education
- [ ] D. D. The Impact of Automation

### Q4 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：根据文章内容选择最佳答案。
文章节选：The study found that students who read for pleasure scored higher on standardized tests than those who did not. This suggests that reading habits have a significant impact on academic performance.
问题：What is the main finding of the study?

- [x] A. A. Reading for pleasure improves test scores.
- [ ] B. B. Standardized tests are difficult for most students.
- [ ] C. C. Academic performance is unrelated to reading habits.
- [ ] D. D. Students should read only textbooks.

### Q5 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：根据文章内容选择最佳答案。
文章节选：The company's new policy of flexible working hours resulted in a 20% increase in productivity. Employees reported higher job satisfaction and lower stress levels.
问题：What was the effect of the new policy?

- [ ] A. A. It decreased productivity.
- [ ] B. B. It increased employee stress.
- [x] C. C. It improved productivity and satisfaction.
- [ ] D. D. It had no noticeable effect.
