### 仔细阅读·词义题练习

#### 核心知识点
词义题考查考生根据上下文推断生词或短语含义的能力。解题关键在于**定位线索句**，利用**逻辑关系**（因果、转折、并列、解释）或**同义复现**推断词义。不依赖词汇量，依赖语境分析。

#### 重点难点
- **上下文线索识别**：注意指示词如 `because`、`however`、`in other words`、`such as`。
- **代词的指代**：`it`、`they`、`this` 等代词常指向被考查词的上文。
- **同义替换**：选项常以同义或反义短语替换原文表达，需匹配语义而非字面。

#### 解题步骤
1. **定位**：找到题干中词义题对应的原文句子。
2. **扫描上下文**：前后各1-2句，寻找解释、举例、对比或因果词。
3. **排除干扰**：排除与原文无关或过度推断的选项，选择最贴近语境的释义。

### 练习：仔细阅读词义题

**Passage 1**  
The concept of **gamification** has been widely adopted in education. By incorporating game elements like points, badges, and leaderboards, teachers aim to increase student engagement. However, critics argue that this approach may **undermine** intrinsic motivation, as students might focus on rewards rather than learning itself.

1. The word "**gamification**" in the passage most probably means ______.  
   A. the design of video games  
   B. the use of game-like features in non-game contexts  
   C. the process of playing educational games  
   D. the competition among students  

2. The word "**undermine**" is closest in meaning to ______.  
   A. strengthen  
   B. weaken  
   C. ignore  
   D. replace  

**Passage 2**  
The rapid urbanization in developing countries has led to a surge in **slum** populations. These areas often lack basic infrastructure, such as clean water and sanitation. Yet, some economists view slums as **incubators** of entrepreneurship, where residents create innovative solutions to daily challenges.

3. The word "**slum**" refers to ______.  
   A. a wealthy residential area  
   B. a densely populated, impoverished urban area  
   C. a government-built housing project  
   D. a rural settlement  

4. The word "**incubators**" in the context means ______.  
   A. places where eggs are hatched  
   B. environments that foster development  
   C. medical devices for premature babies  
   D. educational institutions

### 翻译题（中译英）

将下列段落翻译成英语：

“近年来，共享经济在中国迅速发展，涵盖了从共享单车到共享住宿的多个领域。这种模式不仅提高了资源利用效率，还创造了大量就业机会。然而，监管滞后和安全问题也日益凸显。”

**参考译文**：  
In recent years, the sharing economy has developed rapidly in China, covering multiple sectors from shared bikes to shared accommodation. This model not only improves resource efficiency but also creates a large number of job opportunities. However, regulatory lag and safety issues have also become increasingly prominent.
