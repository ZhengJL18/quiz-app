### 核心知识点

**中国特色词汇**指反映中国特有政治、经济、文化、社会现象的词汇，翻译时需兼顾**语义准确**与**文化适应性**。常见策略包括：

- **直译**：保留原词结构，如“一带一路” → Belt and Road Initiative  
- **意译**：解释核心含义，如“脱贫攻坚” → fight against poverty  
- **拼音+解释**：如“广场舞” → square dancing (guangchangwu)  
- **缩略语**：如“双循环” → dual circulation  

### 重点难点

1. **政治术语**：需注意官方标准译法，如“中国梦” → Chinese Dream（不可译为 China Dream）  
2. **文化负载词**：避免字面直译导致误解，如“红包” → red packet（而非 red envelope）  
3. **四字结构**：中文凝练，英文需补充逻辑，如“绿水青山就是金山银山” → lucid waters and lush mountains are invaluable assets  
4. **时态与单复数**：中文无形态变化，英文需根据语境调整，如“改革开放” → reform and opening-up（固定搭配，无复数）  

### 简单示例

| 中文 | 英文 | 策略 |
|------|------|------|
| 高铁 | high-speed rail | 直译 |
| 乡村振兴 | rural revitalization | 意译 |
| 支付宝 | Alipay | 专有名词 |
| 舌尖上的中国 | A Bite of China | 意译+文化适配 |

### 练习题（贴合四六级难度）

#### 一、阅读理解

**选词填空**（从方框中选择正确单词，每个限用一次）  
词汇：initiative, prosperity, heritage, poverty, emission  

China has achieved a historic milestone in lifting nearly 100 million people out of ___ (1) ___. The country’s ___ (2) ___ reduction target has been met ahead of schedule. Meanwhile, the Belt and Road ___ (3) ___ promotes global economic ___ (4) ___.  

**长篇匹配**（将段落与主题匹配）  
A. 5G technology and digital economy  
B. Traditional Chinese medicine (TCM)  
C. Intangible cultural heritage protection  

段落1：The State Council has listed shadow puppetry as a national intangible cultural heritage, requiring local governments to establish training centers for young inheritors.  
→ 匹配主题：___  

**仔细阅读**（单选）  
“Dual circulation” refers to a development pattern where domestic and international markets reinforce each other, with the domestic market as the mainstay. This strategy aims to boost internal consumption while maintaining openness to global trade.  

Q: What is the primary role of the domestic market in the “dual circulation” pattern?  
A. It replaces international trade entirely.  
B. It serves as the foundation of the strategy.  
C. It focuses only on high-tech products.  
D. It reduces reliance on foreign investments.  

#### 二、翻译（中译英段落）

**话题：科技与社会**  
中国数字支付普及率全球领先，移动支付交易额连续五年位居世界第一。然而，老年人群体面临“数字鸿沟”，部分公共场所仍保留现金支付渠道以保障其权益。  

参考答案：  
China leads the world in digital payment adoption, with mobile payment transaction volume ranking first globally for five consecutive years. However, the elderly face a “digital divide,” and some public venues still retain cash payment channels to safeguard their rights.
