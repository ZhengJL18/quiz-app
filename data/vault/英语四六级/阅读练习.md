## 英语四六级·阅读练习

### 一、核心知识点

**阅读理解**分为三大题型：选词填空、长篇匹配、仔细阅读。**翻译题**为中译英段落翻译，侧重文化、经济、科技、社会类话题。

#### 1. 选词填空
- 考查词汇辨析与语法搭配。
- 先标词性（名词、动词、形容词、副词），再根据上下文语法结构选择。
- 注意固定搭配（如 `take advantage of`）和词形变化（过去式、复数等）。

#### 2. 长篇匹配
- 考查快速定位与信息匹配。
- 先读题干，划出关键词（专有名词、数字、大写词）。
- 扫读原文段落首尾句，匹配同义替换（如 `increase` ↔ `rise`）。

#### 3. 仔细阅读
- 考查细节理解、推理判断、主旨概括。
- 问题顺序通常对应文章段落顺序。
- 选项中的绝对词（`all`, `never`）多为干扰项。

#### 4. 翻译（中译英）
- 忠实原文，不增不减。
- 注意时态、主谓一致、连接词使用。
- 遇到文化词（如“春节”）用拼音加解释：`Spring Festival (Chinese New Year)`。

---

### 二、重点难点

- **选词填空**：一词多义（如 `address` 可作“地址”或“处理”）。
- **长篇匹配**：同义替换隐蔽（如 `difficult` → `challenging`）。
- **仔细阅读**：推理题需基于原文，不凭常识。
- **翻译**：长句拆分，避免中式英语。

---

### 三、简单示例

#### 选词填空示例
原文：The company decided to ______ a new marketing strategy.  
选项：A. adopt  B. adapt  C. adept  
解析：`adopt` 意为“采用”，符合语境。`adapt` 意为“适应”，`adept` 是形容词“熟练的”。

#### 长篇匹配示例
题干：The study found that sleep deprivation affects memory.  
原文段落首句：A recent research indicates that lack of sleep impairs cognitive functions, especially memory retention.  
匹配：`sleep deprivation` ↔ `lack of sleep`，`affects memory` ↔ `impairs...memory retention`。

#### 仔细阅读示例
问题：What does the author imply about online learning?  
原文：Online learning offers flexibility, but it requires strong self-discipline.  
答案：It has both advantages and challenges.

#### 翻译示例
原文：春节是中国最重要的传统节日，家人团聚、吃年夜饭是核心习俗。  
翻译：The Spring Festival (Chinese New Year) is the most important traditional festival in China, with family reunions and the New Year's Eve dinner as core customs.

---

**练习提示**：限时完成，选词填空8分钟，长篇匹配15分钟，仔细阅读每篇10分钟，翻译15分钟。



### Q1 (fill_blank)
选词填空：选择正确的词填入空白处。The government has taken measures to ______ economic growth in rural areas. (选项：A. stimulate B. substitute C. simulate D. stipulate)

答案：A

### Q2 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：According to the passage, what is the main reason for the decline in traditional media?

- [x] A. A. The rise of digital platforms.
- [ ] B. B. The lack of government support.
- [ ] C. C. The high cost of production.
- [ ] D. D. The decrease in advertising revenue.

### Q3 (multiple_choice)
长篇匹配：Which of the following statements about climate change are supported by the passage? (多选)

- [x] A. A. It is mainly caused by human activities.
- [x] B. B. It has led to an increase in natural disasters.
- [ ] C. C. It can be completely reversed within a decade.
- [x] D. D. It affects both developed and developing countries.

### Q4 (short_answer)
翻译题：将以下中文段落翻译成英文。

近年来，中国在人工智能领域取得了显著进展，许多技术已应用于医疗、交通和教育等行业。


### Q5 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：What does the author imply about online learning in Paragraph 3?

- [ ] A. A. It is less effective than traditional learning.
- [x] B. B. It requires more self-discipline from students.
- [ ] C. C. It has been widely adopted in all countries.
- [ ] D. D. It eliminates the need for teachers.

### Q6 (fill_blank)
选词填空：The new policy aims to ______ the gap between the rich and the poor. (选项：A. widen B. narrow C. ignore D. maintain)

答案：B

### Q7 (multiple_choice)
长篇匹配：According to the passage, which factors contribute to the success of a startup? (多选)

- [x] A. A. Adequate funding.
- [x] B. B. A strong team.
- [ ] C. C. Government regulations.
- [x] D. D. Market demand.

### Q8 (short_answer)
翻译题：将以下中文段落翻译成英文。

中国传统文化强调和谐与平衡，这种思想深刻影响了现代社会的价值观和行为方式。
