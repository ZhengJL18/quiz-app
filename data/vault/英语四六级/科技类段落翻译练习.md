### 科技类段落翻译练习

#### 核心知识点
科技类翻译聚焦术语准确性、被动语态处理、长句拆分与逻辑连接。常见话题包括人工智能、信息技术、生物工程、航天探索等。

#### 重点难点
1. **术语统一**：同一概念在中文与英文中需对应标准术语，如“人工智能”译为“artificial intelligence (AI)”，不可意译。
2. **被动语态**：中文多用主动，英文科技文本偏好被动。如“该技术被广泛应用于医疗”译为“This technology is widely applied in medical treatment.”
3. **长句拆分**：中文多流水句，英译需明确主谓宾，用从句或分词结构重组。如“随着数据量增长，算法性能显著提升”译为“As the amount of data grows, the algorithm performance has improved significantly.”
4. **逻辑显化**：中文隐含因果关系，英文需显性连接词。如“芯片体积缩小，功耗降低”译为“As the chip size shrinks, power consumption decreases.”

#### 示例练习

**1. 选词填空**（从以下选项中选择正确词形填入空格）
选项：A. breakthrough  B. accelerate  C. implementation  D. sustainable  E. integrate

> The recent ______ in quantum computing will ______ the development of new materials. To achieve a ______ future, scientists must ______ these technologies into existing systems. The ______ of such solutions requires cross-disciplinary collaboration.

**2. 长篇匹配**（将左侧句子与右侧段落匹配）
- ① Cloud computing enables remote data processing.
- ② Edge computing reduces latency.
- ③ 5G networks support massive IoT connections.
- A. Paragraph A: In traditional architectures, data is sent to centralized servers. This creates delays for real-time applications. By processing data near the source, edge computing achieves response times under 10 milliseconds.
- B. Paragraph B: The fifth-generation mobile network can handle up to one million devices per square kilometer, making it ideal for smart city sensors and autonomous vehicles.
- C. Paragraph C: Users access storage and software over the internet, eliminating the need for local hardware. This model has been adopted by 90% of enterprises for flexibility and cost reduction.

**3. 仔细阅读**
> The transition from 4G to 5G is not merely an upgrade in speed. It represents a paradigm shift in network architecture, enabling ultra-reliable low-latency communications (URLLC) for industrial automation. For instance, remote surgery requires latency below 1 millisecond, which 5G can achieve through network slicing. However, the deployment of small cells in urban areas faces challenges such as high costs and aesthetic concerns.

问题：According to the passage, what makes 5G suitable for industrial automation?
A. Higher download speeds  
B. Ultra-reliable low-latency communications  
C. Lower infrastructure costs  
D. Wider coverage area  

**4. 中译英段落翻译**
中文原文：
人工智能在医疗领域的应用日益广泛。通过分析大量医学影像，AI系统能够辅助医生检测早期病变。然而，数据隐私和算法偏见仍是亟待解决的问题。研究人员正致力于开发更加透明和公平的AI模型，以确保其在临床实践中的可靠性。

参考译文：
The application of artificial intelligence in the medical field is increasingly widespread. By analyzing large volumes of medical images, AI systems can assist doctors in detecting early lesions. However, data privacy and algorithmic bias remain urgent issues. Researchers are working to develop more transparent and fair AI models to ensure their reliability in clinical practice.

## 练习题

### Q1 (fill_blank)
将下列中文段落翻译成英文：
人工智能正在深刻改变我们的生活。它能够在医疗、交通、教育等领域发挥重要作用，提高效率并降低成本。然而，我们也必须警惕其潜在风险，如隐私泄露和就业替代。

答案：Artificial intelligence is profoundly changing our lives. It can play an important role in fields such as healthcare, transportation, and education, improving efficiency and reducing costs. However, we must also be wary of its potential risks, such as privacy breaches and job displacement.
