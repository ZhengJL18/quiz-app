## 仔细阅读·态度题练习

### 一、核心知识点

态度题考查考生对作者或文中人物观点、立场、情感倾向的判断。常见提问方式包括：
- What is the author’s attitude towards...?
- The author’s tone in this passage is...
- The writer seems to be ______ about...

**解题关键**：寻找态度信号词，如形容词（positive, critical, skeptical）、副词（fortunately, surprisingly）、情态动词（should, must）、转折词（but, however）。

### 二、重点难点

**难点1：区分客观陈述与主观态度。** 作者可能先陈述事实，再表达观点。需定位态度词所在句，而非仅看首尾句。

**难点2：识别隐含态度。** 无直接态度词时，通过选词褒贬性判断。如“outdated system”含贬义，“innovative approach”含褒义。

**难点3：排除干扰项。** 常见错误选项如“indifferent”（冷漠，若作者有观点则排除）、“ambiguous”（模糊，若态度明确则排除）。

### 三、示例

**Passage excerpt**:
> While the government claims the policy has boosted economic growth, many economists argue that the benefits have been unevenly distributed. The rising income gap, they warn, could undermine long-term social stability.

**Question**:
What is the economists’ attitude toward the policy’s outcome?

A. Positive  
B. Skeptical  
C. Indifferent  
D. Enthusiastic  

**解析**：
- 定位信号词：economists argue（争论），warn（警告），unevenly distributed（分配不均），undermine（破坏）——均为负面或谨慎表述。
- 排除：A（积极）、D（热情）与原文矛盾；C（冷漠）与“argue/warn”的参与态度矛盾。
- 正确答案：B（怀疑的）。作者通过“argue”“warn”表明经济学家对政策效果持保留态度。

### 四、练习建议

1. 通读题干，明确问的是作者还是文中某人态度。
2. 快速扫描态度词所在句，标记褒贬或中立信号。
3. 若选项含“indifferent”或“neutral”，先检查作者是否确实未表态。
4. 避免过度推理，选项需有原文依据。

## 练习题

### Q1 (single_choice)
Passage: The author's primary purpose in writing this article is to...

The author's attitude towards the new policy can best be described as:

- [x] A. A. Critical
- [ ] B. B. Supportive
- [ ] C. C. Neutral
- [ ] D. D. Ambiguous

### Q2 (single_choice)
Passage: The researcher's tone in discussing the experiment results is one of...

What is the author's attitude towards the findings?

- [ ] A. A. Skeptical
- [ ] B. B. Enthusiastic
- [x] C. C. Objective
- [ ] D. D. Disappointed

### Q3 (single_choice)
Passage: The writer expresses strong disapproval of the current education system.

Which word best describes the author's attitude?

- [ ] A. A. Indifferent
- [x] B. B. Critical
- [ ] C. C. Optimistic
- [ ] D. D. Curious

### Q4 (single_choice)
Passage: The author argues that technological progress has brought more benefits than drawbacks.

The author's attitude towards technology is:

- [x] A. A. Positive
- [ ] B. B. Negative
- [ ] C. C. Neutral
- [ ] D. D. Uncertain

### Q5 (single_choice)
Passage: While acknowledging the challenges, the author remains hopeful about the future.

The author's overall tone can be described as:

- [ ] A. A. Pessimistic
- [x] B. B. Cautiously optimistic
- [ ] C. C. Indifferent
- [ ] D. D. Sarcastic

### Q6 (single_choice)
Passage: The article concludes that the government's response to the crisis was inadequate.

The author's attitude towards the government's actions is:

- [ ] A. A. Approving
- [x] B. B. Critical
- [ ] C. C. Unconcerned
- [ ] D. D. Confused

### Q7 (single_choice)
Passage: The author presents both sides of the argument without taking a clear stance.

The tone of the passage is mainly:

- [ ] A. A. Biased
- [x] B. B. Objective
- [ ] C. C. Emotional
- [ ] D. D. Humorous

### Q8 (single_choice)
Passage: The writer uses words like 'disastrous' and 'tragic' to describe the environmental situation.

The author's attitude towards the environment is:

- [x] A. A. Concerned
- [ ] B. B. Indifferent
- [ ] C. C. Optimistic
- [ ] D. D. Joyful
