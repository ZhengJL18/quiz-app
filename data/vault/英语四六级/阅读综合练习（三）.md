### 阅读综合练习（三）——核心知识点与示例

#### 一、核心知识点

1. **选词填空**：基于词性（名词、动词、形容词、副词）和上下文逻辑（因果、转折、并列）快速定位。重点掌握**固定搭配**（如 `take advantage of`）和**词形变化**（如 `-tion` 表名词，`-ly` 表副词）。
2. **长篇匹配**：先读题干，提取**专有名词、数字、核心动词**作为定位词。扫描段落首尾句，匹配同义替换（如 `prosperity` → `economic growth`）。
3. **仔细阅读**：**细节题**回原文定位，排除绝对化选项（`always`、`never`）；**主旨题**关注首段末句或尾段首句；**推断题**选择逻辑合理、原文未直接明说的选项。
4. **段落翻译**：中译英需注意**主谓一致**、**时态**（一般现在时描述事实，现在完成时表影响）和**被动语态**（科技、经济类常用）。文化类词汇保留拼音或意译（如“春节” → `Spring Festival`）。

#### 二、重点难点

- **选词填空**：区分近义词（如 `effect` 与 `affect` 的词性差异）。
- **长篇匹配**：题干与原文**同义替换**的识别（如 `reduce` → `cut down`）。
- **仔细阅读**：**长难句**主干提取（去掉插入语、定语从句）。
- **翻译**：**无主句**补主语（如“必须采取措施” → `Measures must be taken`）；**四字短语**简化（如“日新月异” → `changing rapidly`）。

#### 三、简单示例

**1. 选词填空**  
原文：The new policy aims to ______ the gap between urban and rural education.  
选项：A. narrow  B. narrows  C. narrowing  D. narrowly  
解析：`aims to` 后接动词原形，选 A. narrow。

**2. 长篇匹配**  
题干：The impact of technology on traditional jobs is often underestimated.  
段落首句：Many experts argue that automation’s effect on employment is not fully recognized.  
匹配：`underestimated` 与 `not fully recognized` 同义替换，匹配成功。

**3. 仔细阅读**  
问题：What can be inferred about the company’s strategy?  
原文：The company shifted focus to renewable energy, though profits dropped initially.  
答案：The company prioritized long-term gains over short-term profits.

**4. 翻译**  
中文：随着人工智能的发展，许多重复性工作将被机器人取代。  
英文：With the development of artificial intelligence, many repetitive jobs will be replaced by robots.  
注意：被动语态 `will be replaced` 和“随着”对应 `With`。

## 练习题

### Q1 (single_choice)
What is the main idea of the passage? The passage discusses the impact of social media on interpersonal communication, arguing that while it enhances connectivity, it often reduces the depth of face-to-face interactions.

- [ ] A. A. Social media completely destroys interpersonal relationships.
- [ ] B. B. Social media improves the quality of all communications.
- [x] C. C. Social media increases connectivity but may weaken deep interactions.
- [ ] D. D. Social media has no effect on face-to-face communication.

### Q2 (single_choice)
According to the passage, what is one advantage of online learning mentioned? The author states that online learning provides flexibility for students who cannot attend traditional classes due to work or family commitments.

- [ ] A. A. It is always cheaper than traditional education.
- [x] B. B. It offers flexibility for students with other commitments.
- [ ] C. C. It guarantees higher grades than classroom learning.
- [ ] D. D. It eliminates the need for teachers entirely.

### Q3 (fill_blank)
Complete the sentence: The rapid development of artificial intelligence has brought significant changes to various industries, _______ (particularly) in the field of healthcare, where AI is used to diagnose diseases more accurately.

答案：particularly

### Q4 (multiple_choice)
Which of the following factors are mentioned as causes of climate change in the passage? (Select all that apply.)

- [x] A. A. Industrial emissions
- [x] B. B. Deforestation
- [ ] C. C. Volcanic eruptions
- [ ] D. D. Overfishing

### Q5 (short_answer)
Translate the following Chinese sentence into English: 随着电子商务的快速发展，越来越多的消费者倾向于在线购物，这极大地改变了传统的零售模式。


### Q6 (single_choice)
In the passage, the word 'ubiquitous' in the sentence 'Smartphones have become ubiquitous in modern society' most probably means:

- [ ] A. A. rare
- [ ] B. B. expensive
- [x] C. C. everywhere
- [ ] D. D. useless

### Q7 (fill_blank)
The author argues that the key to solving the urban traffic problem is to invest more in public transportation, such as buses and subways, which can _______ (显著地) reduce the number of private cars on the road.

答案：significantly
