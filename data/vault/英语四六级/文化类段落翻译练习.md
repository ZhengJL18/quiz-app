### 文化类段落翻译练习

**核心知识点**：掌握文化类常见词汇（如“非物质文化遗产”“传统节日”“民俗”），灵活处理中英文句式差异（如汉语主动转英语被动、无主句补主语）。

**重点难点**：
1. **专有名词翻译**：保留文化特色，采用“音译+释义”或直译。例如：“元宵节”译作 *Lantern Festival*，“京剧”译作 *Peking opera*。
2. **句式转换**：汉语多用短句、流水句，英语需合并为复合句或从句。例如：“中国剪纸历史悠久，风格独特”可译为 *Chinese paper-cutting, with a long history and unique style, ...*
3. **时态与语态**：描述文化现象多用一般现在时；客观陈述用被动语态。例如：“茶道被广泛传承”译为 *The tea ceremony is widely inherited*。

---

### 示例练习

**题目**：将以下段落翻译成英文（120-150词）。  
> 中国书法是一种独特的艺术形式。它不仅是一种书写方式，更是一种表达情感与精神境界的手段。练习书法需要长期坚持，注重笔画的力度与结构的平衡。如今，书法已被列入联合国教科文组织非物质文化遗产名录，吸引了越来越多的海外爱好者。

**参考译文**：  
Chinese calligraphy is a unique art form. It is not only a way of writing but also a means of expressing emotions and spiritual realms. Practicing calligraphy requires long-term persistence, with emphasis on the strength of strokes and the balance of structure. Nowadays, calligraphy has been inscribed on the UNESCO Intangible Cultural Heritage List, attracting an increasing number of overseas enthusiasts.

---

### 自学要点
- **词汇积累**：整理文化类高频词（如 *heritage*, *ritual*, *folk art*），制作中英对照表。
- **句型模板**：掌握“不仅……而且……”（*not only...but also...*）、“随着……”（*with the development of...*）等结构。
- **检查清单**：翻译后核对专有名词大小写、时态一致性、主谓单复数。

## 练习题

### Q1 (fill_blank)
在选词填空中，根据上下文选择合适的词填空：The Great Wall, as a symbol of Chinese ancient civilization, has been listed as a World Heritage site by UNESCO. It was originally built for ______ (防御) purposes.

答案：defensive

### Q2 (single_choice)
阅读下列段落，选择最佳答案：The Dragon Boat Festival, celebrated on the fifth day of the fifth lunar month, commemorates the death of the poet Qu Yuan. It is known for dragon boat races and eating zongzi. What is the main purpose of the festival?

- [ ] A. A. To celebrate the harvest
- [x] B. B. To honor a poet
- [ ] C. C. To welcome spring
- [ ] D. D. To worship dragons

### Q3 (single_choice)
仔细阅读以下段落并回答问题：Tea culture in China dates back thousands of years. It is not only a beverage but also a way of social interaction. Tea ceremonies emphasize harmony and respect. According to the passage, what does tea culture primarily reflect?

- [ ] A. A. Economic development
- [x] B. B. Social values
- [ ] C. C. Agricultural techniques
- [ ] D. D. Artistic styles

### Q4 (single_choice)
长篇匹配题：将下列文化现象与对应的描述匹配。选项：A. 春节 B. 中秋节 C. 清明节 D. 端午节。描述：A time for family reunion and moon watching.

- [ ] A. A. 春节
- [x] B. B. 中秋节
- [ ] C. C. 清明节
- [ ] D. D. 端午节
