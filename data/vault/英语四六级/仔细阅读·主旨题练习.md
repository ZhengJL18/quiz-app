### 仔细阅读·主旨题练习

**核心知识点**：主旨题考查对全文中心思想或作者主要观点的把握，通常出现在文章首尾段、段落首尾句、转折词后或重复出现的概念中。  
**解题策略**：  
- 快速浏览首段、尾段及各段首句，识别高频词或同义复现。  
- 排除细节干扰：选项若为具体例子、数据或某一侧面观点，则非主旨。  
- 选项需概括全文，而非仅某一段落。

**重点难点**：  
- 混淆主旨与细节：易错选与原文一致但仅覆盖局部信息的选项。  
- 隐含主旨：当文章无明显主题句时，需综合各段逻辑推断。  
- 选项表述过宽或过窄：需对比选项的抽象程度与文章覆盖范围。

**简单示例**  
文章（节选）：  
> Climate change is accelerating ice melt in polar regions, disrupting ecosystems and raising sea levels. While some species adapt, many face extinction. Governments have pledged emission cuts, but progress remains slow.  

问题：What is the main idea of the passage?  
A) Polar ice melt is caused by climate change.  
B) Climate change poses multiple threats to polar regions.  
C) Some species can adapt to climate change.  
D) Governments are failing to reduce emissions.  

解析：选项A、C、D均为文中具体细节，B概括了“多重威胁”（冰融、生态破坏、海平面上升、物种危机），符合主旨。正确答案：B。

**练习**（仔细阅读题）  
文章（节选）：  
> The rise of remote work has reshaped urban economies, reducing demand for office space but boosting suburban housing markets. Commute times have dropped, yet employees report increased isolation. Companies now face challenges in maintaining team cohesion.  

问题：What is the passage mainly about?  
A) The decline of office space demand.  
B) The impact of remote work on cities and workers.  
C) The rise of suburban housing markets.  
D) The difficulty of maintaining team cohesion.

**答案与解析**  
正确答案：B。解析：A、C、D均为文章中的具体细节，分别对应“办公空间需求下降”、“郊区住房市场升温”、“团队凝聚力困难”。选项B“远程工作对城市和工人的影响”概括了全文多维度内容，符合主旨。

- [ ] B. the history of social media development

- [ ] D. the benefits of social media for education

- [ ] D. To persuade readers to adopt online learning.
