## 仔细阅读·推断题练习

**核心知识点**：推断题要求考生基于原文信息进行合理推论，而非直接照搬原文表述。答案通常隐含在字里行间，需结合上下文逻辑、作者态度或因果关系推导。

**重点难点**：
- **区分事实与推断**：选项若为原文直接陈述，则为事实题，非推断题。
- **避免过度推断**：推论必须基于原文证据，不能加入个人主观假设。
- **识别干扰项**：常见干扰项包括“无中生有”“偷换概念”“反向推断”。

**解题步骤**：
1. 定位题干关键词，找到原文对应句。
2. 分析该句的隐含意义（如转折、因果、对比、例证）。
3. 排除与原文矛盾或无关的选项，选择逻辑一致的推论。

---

### 示例练习

**Passage (节选)**  
The rapid urbanization in developing countries has led to a surge in demand for housing, yet the supply of affordable housing remains critically insufficient. Governments often prioritize commercial real estate projects due to higher tax revenue, leaving low-income families with limited options. Some experts argue that without policy intervention, the housing gap will exacerbate social inequality.

**Question**  
What can be inferred about the current housing policies in developing countries?  
A. They focus on providing affordable housing for low-income families.  
B. They favor commercial projects over residential needs.  
C. They have successfully narrowed the housing gap.  
D. They are designed to attract foreign investment.

**解析**  
- 定位句：“Governments often prioritize commercial real estate projects due to higher tax revenue.”  
- 推论：政府优先商业项目，说明政策倾向商业而非住宅，且未提及低收入家庭住房。  
- 排除A（与原文矛盾）、C（原文指出“supply insufficient”）、D（未提及外资）。  
- **正确答案**：B

### 翻译题（中译英）

**原句**：随着人工智能技术的普及，许多传统岗位面临被替代的风险，但同时也催生了数据分析师等新兴职业。

**参考译文**：With the proliferation of artificial intelligence technology, many traditional jobs are at risk of being replaced, yet it has also given rise to emerging professions such as data analysts.

## 练习题

### Q1 (single_choice)
According to the passage, which of the following can be inferred about the author's attitude towards social media?

- [ ] A. A. It is entirely positive.
- [ ] B. B. It is cautiously optimistic.
- [x] C. C. It is critical but balanced.
- [ ] D. D. It is completely negative.

### Q2 (single_choice)
What can be inferred from the last paragraph about the relationship between economic growth and environmental protection?

- [ ] A. A. They are mutually exclusive.
- [x] B. B. They can be balanced through innovation.
- [ ] C. C. Economic growth always harms the environment.
- [ ] D. D. Environmental protection hinders development.

### Q3 (multiple_choice)
Which of the following statements can be inferred from the passage about online education? (Choose two.)

- [ ] A. A. It is less effective than traditional education.
- [x] B. B. It provides flexibility for learners.
- [x] C. C. It requires strong self-discipline.
- [ ] D. D. It is only suitable for technical subjects.

### Q4 (single_choice)
The author mentions the example of a small business owner to suggest that:

- [ ] A. A. Small businesses are more innovative than large corporations.
- [x] B. B. Adaptability is key to surviving economic changes.
- [ ] C. C. Government support is essential for small businesses.
- [ ] D. D. Technology is a threat to traditional businesses.

### Q5 (single_choice)
What can be inferred about the role of cultural exchange in international relations according to the passage?

- [ ] A. A. It is less important than economic cooperation.
- [x] B. B. It helps build mutual understanding.
- [ ] C. C. It often leads to cultural conflicts.
- [ ] D. D. It is a recent development in history.

### Q6 (multiple_choice)
Based on the passage, which factors are implied to contribute to a person's career success? (Choose two.)

- [ ] A. A. High academic grades.
- [x] B. B. Strong networking skills.
- [x] C. C. Continuous learning.
- [ ] D. D. Family background.

### Q7 (single_choice)
The passage implies that the rapid development of artificial intelligence may:

- [ ] A. A. Completely replace human workers.
- [x] B. B. Create new job opportunities.
- [ ] C. C. Only benefit large companies.
- [ ] D. D. Reduce the need for education.

### Q8 (single_choice)
What can be inferred about the author's view on traditional values in modern society?

- [ ] A. A. They are outdated and should be abandoned.
- [x] B. B. They still play a significant role.
- [ ] C. C. They are only relevant in rural areas.
- [ ] D. D. They should be enforced by law.
