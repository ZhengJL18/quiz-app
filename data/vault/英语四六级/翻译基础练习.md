### 翻译基础练习

#### 核心知识点
翻译是四六级考试中考查语言综合运用能力的题型，要求将中文段落准确、通顺地译成英文。核心在于**理解原文**、**选择恰当词汇**与**构建正确句式**。常见考点包括：**时态一致**、**主谓一致**、**被动语态**、**从句结构**以及**固定搭配**。

#### 重点难点
1. **长难句拆分**：中文多短句，英文需合理合并为复合句。
2. **文化负载词处理**：如“端午节”“一带一路”，须采用**音译+解释**或**意译**。
3. **逻辑连接**：中文隐性逻辑（如因果、转折）需用连词显性化。
4. **语态转换**：中文主动语态常转为英文被动语态（如“人们认为” → It is believed that...）。

#### 示例与练习

##### 一、选词填空（选词填空）
**原文**：The rapid development of AI technology has ______ (A. revolutionized / B. revolution) many industries.  
**答案**：A. revolutionized（动词过去分词，构成完成时态）

##### 二、长篇匹配（段落匹配）
**原文段落**：Economic growth in emerging markets is driven by digital innovation.  
**匹配选项**：  
- 选项1：Digitalization boosts economic development in developing countries.  
- 选项2：Traditional industries are declining due to automation.  
**答案**：选项1（核心词“digital innovation”与“digitalization”对应）

##### 三、仔细阅读（阅读理解）
**原文**：Despite concerns about job displacement, AI has created new roles in data analysis and machine learning.  
**问题**：What is the author’s attitude toward AI’s impact on employment?  
**答案**：Balanced / Objective（原文同时提及“concerns”和“new roles”）

##### 四、段落翻译（中译英）
**中文**：近年来，中国大力发展可再生能源，如风能和太阳能，以减少对化石燃料的依赖。  
**参考译文**：In recent years, China has vigorously developed renewable energy sources, such as wind and solar power, to reduce its reliance on fossil fuels.  
**解析**：  
- 时态：使用现在完成时（has developed）表示持续至今的动作。  
- 词汇：“大力发展”译为“vigorously developed”，“对……的依赖”译为“reliance on”。  
- 结构：“以减少……”用不定式表目的。

**练习**：  
**中文**：端午节是中国传统节日之一，人们通过赛龙舟和吃粽子来纪念屈原。  
**参考译文**：The Dragon Boat Festival is one of China’s traditional festivals, during which people commemorate Qu Yuan by racing dragon boats and eating zongzi.  
**解析**：  
- 文化词：“端午节”译为“Dragon Boat Festival”（意译），“粽子”音译加注“zongzi”。  
- 从句：用“during which”引导定语从句，连接前后分句。

## 练习题

### Q1 (fill_blank)
选词填空：在以下句子中填入正确的词汇形式。The rapid development of artificial intelligence has ________ (transform) many industries, including healthcare and finance.

答案：transformed

### Q2 (single_choice)
长篇匹配：阅读以下段落，选择最适合的标题。Paragraph: In recent years, China has invested heavily in renewable energy, such as solar and wind power, to reduce carbon emissions. This shift not only helps protect the environment but also creates new job opportunities in green industries.

- [ ] A. A. The Decline of Fossil Fuels in China
- [x] B. B. China's Investment in Renewable Energy and Its Benefits
- [ ] C. C. Environmental Problems Caused by Industrialization
- [ ] D. D. The Global Impact of Carbon Emissions

### Q3 (single_choice)
仔细阅读：根据短文内容选择正确答案。Passage: The Forbidden City in Beijing, a UNESCO World Heritage site, attracts millions of tourists annually. Built in the early 15th century, it served as the imperial palace for 24 emperors. Its architecture reflects traditional Chinese cosmology, with the main halls aligned on a north-south axis.Question: What does the passage mainly discuss?

- [ ] A. A. The number of tourists visiting the Forbidden City
- [x] B. B. The historical and cultural significance of the Forbidden City
- [ ] C. C. The architectural style of Chinese imperial palaces
- [ ] D. D. The role of UNESCO in preserving heritage sites

### Q4 (short_answer)
中译英段落翻译：将以下中文句子翻译成英语。近年来，中国在科技创新方面取得了显著成就，特别是在5G通信和高速铁路领域。这些进步极大地提高了人们的生活质量，并促进了经济的持续增长。


### Q5 (fill_blank)
选词填空：从以下词汇中选择适当的形式填入句子。词汇：promote, culture, global.Chinese tea ________ has a long history and is deeply rooted in daily life. It has also gained ________ recognition as a symbol of hospitality.

答案：culture; global

### Q6 (single_choice)
长篇匹配：阅读以下段落，选择最适合的标题。Paragraph: With the rise of e-commerce platforms like Taobao and JD.com, traditional brick-and-mortar stores are facing increasing pressure. Many small retailers have had to adapt by offering online services or closing down entirely.

- [ ] A. A. The Growth of E-commerce in China
- [ ] B. B. The Decline of Traditional Retail
- [ ] C. C. How Small Retailers Can Survive Online Competition
- [x] D. D. The Impact of E-commerce on Traditional Retail

### Q7 (short_answer)
中译英段落翻译：将以下中文句子翻译成英语。中国的传统节日，如春节和中秋节，不仅是家庭团聚的时刻，也是传承文化的重要方式。这些节日体现了中华民族的价值观和习俗。
